## üìè IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS

### Dimensions Guide
- **Hero/Background Images**: 1920x1080px minimum (landscape)
- **Gallery Full Images**: 1920x1280px minimum (3:2 ratio)
- **Portfolio Thumbnails**: 600x800px minimum (portrait orientation)
- **Featured Images**: 800x600px minimum
- **Team Photos**: 500x500px minimum (square)
- **Logo**: 200-400px wide (transparent PNG)

### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for logos
- **Color Space**: sRGB
- **Quality**: High resolution, professionally shot
- **File Size**: Optimized but not overly compressed
  - Hero images: 200-500KB after optimization
  - Gallery images: 150-400KB after optimization
  - Thumbnails: 50-150KB after optimization

### Quality Guidelines
‚úÖ **DO:**
- Use high-resolution images (minimum requirements above)
- Ensure proper lighting and focus
- Maintain consistent color grading
- Include variety (close-ups, wide shots, details)
- Show authentic emotions and moments

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Use phone camera photos (unless very high quality)
- Submit overly dark or overexposed images
- Include images with watermarks from other photographers
- Use heavily filtered or unrealistic edits
- Submit blurry or out-of-focus images

## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Quality over quantity**: Better to have 8 amazing gallery images than 15 mediocre ones.

2. **Tell a story**: Each gallery should flow from getting ready ‚Üí ceremony ‚Üí celebration.

3. **Variety is key**: Mix wide shots, close-ups, details, and candids in each gallery.

4. **Consistent editing**: All images should have a consistent color grade and style.

5. **Client consent**: Always get written permission to use client wedding photos on your website.

6. **Backup originals**: Keep original high-resolution files before any web optimization.

7. **Optimize for web**: Use tools like Adobe Photoshop, TinyPNG, or ImageOptim to reduce file sizes without losing quality.

8. **Name clearly**: Use descriptive names so you know which image is which (e.g., `gallery-sarah-john-01-getting-ready.jpg`).
