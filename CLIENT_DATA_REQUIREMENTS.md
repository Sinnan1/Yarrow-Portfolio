## üì∑ IMAGE SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Color Space**: sRGB
- **Resolution**: 
  - Hero/Background images: 1920x1080px minimum
  - Gallery images: 1920x1280px minimum
  - Thumbnails: 800x600px minimum
  - Team photos: 500x500px minimum

### Naming Convention
Please name files clearly:
- `hero-main.jpg`
- `about-image-1.jpg`, `about-image-2.jpg`
- `featured-wedding-1.jpg` through `featured-wedding-4.jpg`
- `team-[name].jpg` (e.g., `team-siddharth.jpg`)
- `gallery-[couple-names]-[number].jpg` (e.g., `gallery-reva-zach-01.jpg`)

### Quality Guidelines
- Use high-resolution, professionally shot images
- Ensure proper lighting and focus
- Maintain consistent color grading across related images
- Avoid overly dark or overexposed images
- Include diverse shots (close-ups, wide shots, details, candids)

## ‚ùì FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Do I need to provide all 12 wedding galleries at once?**
A: No! You can start with 4-6 galleries and add more over time. The site will work with fewer galleries.

**Q: What if I don't have professional team photos?**
A: You can use working photos (photos of team members at weddings) or wait to add the team section later.

**Q: Can I use the same images in multiple places?**
A: It's better to use unique images for each section, but some overlap is acceptable (e.g., using a gallery image as a thumbnail).

**Q: What if my wedding descriptions are shorter than 150 words?**
A: That's fine! The word counts are guidelines. Quality matters more than hitting exact word counts.

**Q: Do I need to provide all vendor credits?**
A: Vendor credits are optional but recommended as they add authenticity and can help with SEO and networking.
