## ğŸ’¡ PRO TIPS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Start small**: Don't try to do everything at once. Follow the phases.

2. **Quality matters**: 8 amazing images beat 15 mediocre ones.

3. **Be authentic**: Your real story is more compelling than perfect copy.

4. **Get help**: Ask team members, friends, or hire a copywriter if needed.

5. **Client relationships**: Getting testimonials and permissions is easier if you ask soon after the wedding.

6. **Stay organized**: Use the folder structure provided to keep everything organized.

7. **Backup everything**: Keep copies of all original files before editing.

8. **Take your time**: Rushing leads to mistakes. Plan 4-6 weeks for complete content.
